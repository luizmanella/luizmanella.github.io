About Me

I'm a former professional figure skater who turned Ph.D. student. My research is focused on the integration of optimal transport in different areas of machine learning, specifically in federated learning. I am passionate about all areas of artificial intelligence, how to merge biological phenomenon with artifical methods, and how to apply these methods in robotics.
